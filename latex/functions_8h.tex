Attempts to brew a potion based on a known formula. 

This function takes the potion name from the given tokens (spanning from {\ttfamily index} to {\ttfamily end}), looks up the potion in the {\ttfamily potions} \doxylink{struct_hash_map}{Hash\+Map}, checks if the required ingredients are available in the inventory (from the potion\textquotesingle{}s recipe), and if so, consumes the ingredients and increments the potion count in the inventory.

If the potion formula is unknown or ingredients are insufficient, it prints the appropriate message.
